Electrical Engineering Services in Québec & Canada

SEEN Engineering provides professional electrical engineering services across Québec and Canada, specializing in power distribution, service entrance studies, and utility coordination with Hydro-Québec.

Power Distribution Engineering

Design of low and medium voltage distribution systems, load calculations, equipment sizing, and technical documentation for commercial and residential developments.

Service Entrance Studies

Preparation and coordination of service entrance requests and studies in compliance with Hydro-Québec and applicable electrical codes.

Utility Coordination

Engineering support for coordination with public utilities including technical documentation, load summaries, and submission follow-ups.

Technical Deliverables

  • Single-line diagrams
  • Load summaries and calculation notes
  • Electrical distribution layouts
  • Code compliance reviews
