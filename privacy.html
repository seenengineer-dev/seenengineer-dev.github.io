Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-21

1. Who we are

SEEN Engineering / SEEN Ingénierie provides engineering services in Québec and across Canada. This policy explains how we handle personal information on our website.

2. What we collect

  • Contact form details (name, email, project details, message).
  • Technical data (IP address, browser/device info) for security and analytics, when consent is given.

3. How we use it

  • To respond to inquiries and provide services/quotes.
  • To improve the website and measure performance (analytics), only if you accept cookies.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may use service providers (e.g., Formspree for form delivery, Google Analytics if consent is given) to operate the website.

5. Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes above, or as required by law.

6. Your choices

  • You can decline analytics cookies using the cookie banner.
  • You can request access/correction/deletion of your personal information.

7. Contact

For privacy requests, contact: info@seen-ingenierie.ca
